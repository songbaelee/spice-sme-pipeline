East Africa · Spice Sector · CRS Innovative Finance

SME Lending Pipeline

Countries Funds
Purpose

An internal CRS Innovative Finance tool for tracking and prioritising spice sector SMEs across Uganda, Tanzania, and Madagascar as potential borrowers in a CRS-managed agri-SME lending facility. Not for external distribution.

Data Sources

Company websites, donor programme reports (USAID, EU, IFAD, AVSF), trade databases (ITC Trade Map, Tridge), news coverage, sector association directories (VANEX, TASPA, RCCE), buyer/importer documentation, and AI-assisted research synthesis. All entries reviewed by CRS Innovative Finance team.

Lending Readiness Tiers
Tier 1Investment ready — track record, audited accounts or investor validation, active export sales
Tier 2Near ready — export-capable but needs light TA, certification support, or financial documentation
Tier 3Pre-lending — significant technical assistance or capacity building required before lending is viable
Web Presence / Data Confidence
HighMultiple independent sources — website, third-party reports, and/or investor documentation
MediumCompany website plus some third-party verification
LowSingle or indirect source only — placeholder entries from consultations or directories
Network Affiliations
Uganda: VIEF = USDA/CRS VINES project partner · VANEX = Uganda Vanilla Exporters Association member
Tanzania: TASPA = Tanzania Spices Association member · TZ Spices Label = quality certification/branding standard for export-grade spices (TanTrade)
Madagascar: RCCE = cooperative network member · AVSF = Agronomes et Vétérinaires Sans Frontières programme partner
Limitations

Revenue and capital need figures are estimates based on publicly available data and AI-assisted inference — not audited figures. Tier assignments reflect desk research only and should be validated through direct engagement. Many Tier 3 entries are placeholders from in-country consultations with no public profile confirmed.
